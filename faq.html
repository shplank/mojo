FAQ

Q: Will masks be required?
A: YES. Guests must wear masks at all times in the space. It's okay to lower them briefly for a sip of beverage or a bite of food.
Q: Will we be socially distanced?
A: Not during the ceremony, when we’ll have about 100 people in theater seats. Masks will be required during that time, and we will ensure proper air flow in the space.
Q: What if I’m not vaccinated?
A: Sorry, but for everyone's safety, we cannot have unvaccinated guests at this event.
Q: What about the kids?
A: If they’ll be fully vaccinated by Sept. 25 and you feel comfortable bringing them, do it! Just please let us know when you RSVP.
Q: Can I bring a date?
A: Space is limited, so we need to keep this small. If there’s someone really special in your life who isn’t already invited, let us know. We might be able to make space for them on the guest list.
Q: Is it safe to park my car on the street near the venue?
A: Short answer: Yes. Long answer: It's Hollywood, which means there's often more grit than glamour. But as long as you don't leave precious jewels on your dashboard, it's safe.
Q: Can you accommodate my dietary restrictions?
A: Yep! Be sure to let us know in your RSVP (or email one of us).
Q: Will there be activities in the days before or after the wedding?
A: There won’t be official activities, but for those traveling from out of town, we definitely want to make time to hang out with you. We’ll be in touch about that!
Q: Why so last-minute? Are you pregnant? What kind of backwoods sh*t is this?
A: We blame the pandemic. But then we had an opportunity to use the space we wanted: The Broadwater!
Q: Covid has made everything so complicated. Why do this now?
A: Don't tempt us to elope.
Q: Who made your website?
A: We did! Monica designed it and Joe built it, all in the span of a week. You should hire Joe to build your website. He can do even fancier things with JavaScript.
Q: I still have questions. What do I do?
A: We can help! You can reach Monica at monica.howe@gmail.com and Joe at lankyjoe@gmail.com.
